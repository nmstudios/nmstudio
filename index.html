About Us




we turn ideas into works of art.

Design is Everything. NM studio has a strong vision centered around the importance of aesthetics and user experience. This vision prioritizes the creation of services that are visually appealing, intuitive, and enjoyable to use. The company places a high value on creativity, innovation, and attention to detail in all aspects of its operations, from its architecture, interiors, product design and packaging to branding and marketing. Overall, the company's vision is focused on creating a unique and memorable experience for its customers, with design serving as the foundation for achieving that goal.

Our Services

Architecture

At NM Studio, we believe that architecture is not just about building structures, but it's about creating spaces that connect with the natural world while incorporating modern and sophisticated designs. Our team of architects in Bangalore strives to design spaces that seamlessly blend with the environment, creating a balance between functionality and aesthetics. We understand that every project is unique, and we work closely with our clients to understand their needs and deliver personalized solutions that exceed their expectations.

Product

Product design is a key aspect of our services, as we understand that products play a crucial role in enhancing the functionality and aesthetics of architectural projects. We specialize in designing custom products that are unique to each project, ensuring that they are tailored to the client's specific needs and requirements. Our product design process involves extensive research, conceptualization, and prototyping to ensure that the final product meets our high standards of quality and performance.

Interiors

We aim to provide our clients with a personalized and unique design solution that fits their style, budget, and lifestyle. From residential to commercial projects, we guarantee that our interiors are designed to make a statement, leaving a lasting impression on everyone who experiences them. We believe that modern and sophisticated designs should work seamlessly to create spaces that promote easy movement and access. We take pride in designing spaces that are not only visually appealing but also practical, ensuring that they fulfill their intended purpose.

Branding

At NM Studio, we offer comprehensive branding services that help businesses build a strong and distinctive brand identity. Whether you're starting from scratch or looking to revamp your existing brand, our team of branding experts can guide you through the entire process. Our branding services include building brand identity from the ground up, rebranding, developing branding strategy, designing company logos, social media branding, logo designing, and creating a style guide. Our branding services are tailored to meet the specific needs of each client, and we work closely with them to ensure that their vision is translated into a cohesive and impactful brand identity.

Planning

Planning is the backbone of any successful project, and we prioritize it above all else. Our team works together to create comprehensive and detailed plans for every project we undertake. We carefully consider all aspects of the project, including the client's needs, the site conditions, the budget, and the timeline. Our planning process ensures that we are able to deliver high-quality, innovative designs that meet and exceed our clients' expectations. Trust us to plan and execute your architectural project with precision and expertise.

ART

As an architectural studio, NM Studio understands the significance of art in design. We believe that art can play a transformative role in enhancing the aesthetic appeal and emotional impact. We offer a wide range of art services that cater to diverse needs, including artwork selection and curation, custom art installations, mural design and creation. Our art services are designed to complement and enhance the design, creating a cohesive and harmonious space that inspires and uplifts. We collaborate with local and international artists to source and create art that is unique, timeless, and reflective of the client's vision and values.
