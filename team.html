Our Team

Ar. Neelaksh Mahajan

Architect Neelaksh Mahajan is a renowned figure in the field, known for his exceptional creativity and visionary approach. With a Bachelor's degree in Architecture from the prestigious School of Architecture & Planning at Anna University, Chennai, he has received top-notch education and training from one of the country's premier architectural institutions. Neelaksh is recognized for his innovative design ideas, which reflect his out-of-the-box thinking and ability to push boundaries. He is not only an accomplished architect but also a serial entrepreneur, driven by a vision to establish an architectural and design firm with a global presence and multidisciplinary associations with renowned international consultants. Neelaksh's extensive travels have broadened his perspective, making him well-versed in various facets of architecture, design, and construction. Furthermore, his tech-savvy nature enables him to seamlessly integrate technology into his projects, ensuring cutting-edge solutions for his clients.

Ar. Rajini Itham

Architect Rajini Itham, Studio head at NM Studio, brings extensive knowledge and experience. With a strong educational foundation from prestigious institutions, including Anna University, Chennai, and the Centre for Environmental Planning and Technology, Ahmedabad, Rajini has excelled in leading several architectural projects for nearly 25 years. She is recognized for her expertise in diverse endeavors such as master planning and high-rise developments. Rajini is a fellow of esteemed organizations like the Indian Institute of Architects and the Indian Society of Landscape Architects. She actively contributes to academia, overseeing architecture and design programs and serving on various boards and committees. Rajini's influence extends beyond her professional pursuits, with contributions as a session chair, resource person, and jury member in seminars, workshops, and design awards. Her profound expertise is evident through her publications and interviews in print and digital media, establishing her as a prominent figure in the world of architecture and design.
